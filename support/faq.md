# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Yoink, creator tokens, Early Birds, and trading.

## General Questions

### What is Yoink?

Yoink is a creator capital markets platform on Solana that allows Twitch streamers to launch their own tokens. Fans can buy creator tokens, with the first 20 buyers becoming "Early Birds" who earn passive income from all trading activity.

### Do I need crypto experience?

No! Yoink is designed for beginners:
* Simple wallet connection

* 24/7 support
* Active community

### What blockchain does Yoink use?

**Solana** - chosen for:
* Fast transactions (~400ms)
* Low fees (~$0.0001)

### How much does it cost to start?

**Minimum**: ~0.1 SOL ($20-30)
* Covers first trade + fees
* More SOL = more opportunities
* Recommended: 0.5-1 SOL to start

## Trading Questions

### How do I buy tokens?

1. Connect Solana wallet
2. Browse tokens or search creator
3. Enter SOL amount to spend
4. Review details and confirm
5. Approve in wallet
6. Tokens arrive instantly

[Complete buying guide â†’](../traders/how-to-buy.md)

### What are trading fees?

**5% on all trades**, distributed:
* 40% to Creator
* 40% to Early Birds (20 holders)
* 20% to Treasury (buybacks & operations)

### Can I sell anytime?

**Yes** - bonding curve provides instant liquidity. BUT if you're an Early Bird, selling forfeits your seat permanently.

### What is price impact?

Price impact is how much your trade moves the market price. Larger trades have bigger impact. Yoink shows this before you confirm.

### What's a good slippage setting?

* Small trades: 0.5-1%
* Medium trades: 1-3%
* Large trades: 3-10%
* Low liquidity: 5-15%

## Early Bird Questions

### What is an Early Bird?

The first 20 unique buyers of any token who receive a permanent status that earns passive income from ALL trading volume - but ONLY if they never sell.

[Complete Early Bird guide â†’](../traders/early-bird-bonus.md)

### How much can Early Birds earn?

Depends on trading volume:
* Low volume: 0.5-2 SOL/month
* Medium volume: 5-15 SOL/month
* High volume: 20-100+ SOL/month

Each of the 20 Early Birds earns equally.

### Can I have multiple Early Bird seats?

Yes! You can be an Early Bird for multiple different tokens simultaneously.

### What happens if I sell as an Early Bird?

**You lose the seat permanently**:
* âŒ Forfeit status immediately
* âŒ Stop earning rewards
* âŒ Cannot reclaim ever
* âŒ Seat stays empty (not given to someone else)

### How do I know if Early Bird seats are available?

Token pages show "Early Birds: X/20" - if less than 20, seats available!

### Does buying more tokens help as Early Bird?

**For seat**: No, amount doesn't matter for seat
**For earnings**: No, all 20 split equally
**For position**: Yes, you hold more tokens (value increase)

## Technical Questions

### Which wallets work with Yoink?

**Supported**:
* Phantom (recommended)
* Solflare
* Backpack
* Glow
* Most Solana wallets

### Do I need SOL for gas fees?

Yes, keep ~0.01-0.1 SOL for transaction fees. Solana gas is extremely cheap (~$0.0001 per transaction).

### What if transaction fails?

Common causes:
* Insufficient SOL for gas
* Slippage tolerance too low
* Network congestion

Solutions:
* Add more SOL
* Increase slippage
* Wait and retry

### Can I trade from mobile?

Yes! Yoink is fully responsive. Mobile wallets like Phantom work perfectly.

### What happens during Solana network issues?

Transactions may slow but funds are safe. Platform shows network status. Wait for network recovery.

## Token Economics

### What is a bonding curve?

Automated market maker using a mathematical formula to price tokens based on supply. Provides instant liquidity without traditional liquidity pools.

### What does "graduated" mean?

When a token reaches ~10K SOL market cap, it migrates to Raydium DEX for deeper liquidity while maintaining Yoink integration.

### What is YOINK token?

The platform token providing:
* Fee discounts (up to 50%)
