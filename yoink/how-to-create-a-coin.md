# How to Create a Coin

Creating your own token on Yoink is fast, simple, and powerful. Launch your creator coin in under 60 seconds with just a few clicks - no coding required!


<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/create.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>


## Required Information

### Token Details

**Token Name** - Your token's full name (max 32 characters)  
**Token Symbol** - Trading ticker like "CYBER" or "GAME" (max 10 characters, uppercase only)  
**Description** - Brief explanation of your token (max 500 characters)  
**Image** - Square logo image (JPG/PNG, max 5MB, 512x512px recommended)

{% hint style="warning" %}
Choose carefully - token details cannot be changed after creation.
{% endhint %}

## Creation Steps

### 1. Review Everything

Double-check all details before creating - they're permanent!

### 2. Create & Pay

- Click "Create Coin" 
- Approve wallet transaction
- Wait 30-60 seconds for confirmation


<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/done.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>


### 3. Start Trading

Your coin goes live immediately with:
- Bonding curve trading enabled
- Your token page URL to share
- Integration with Yoink's ecosystem
