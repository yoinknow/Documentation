# How to Attach a Stream

Connect your Twitch stream to your token for real-time engagement and live trading activity. Streaming tokens get special visibility and help build stronger communities around your project.

## Two Ways to Stream

### Option 1: Fork Existing Twitch Stream (During Token Creation)

When creating your token, you can connect an existing Twitch stream:

1. **Toggle on "Attach a Twitch stream"** during coin creation
2. **Enter your stream title** - this becomes your token's main title
3. **Add your Twitch URL** (optional) - e.g., `https://twitch.tv/yourusername`
4. **Click the refresh button** to auto-load live stream information
5. **Create your token** - stream integration happens immediately

**Benefits:**
- Real-time thumbnail updates when you're live
- "LIVE" badge appears on your token page
- Direct viewer traffic from token traders to your stream
- Increased visibility in Yoink's streaming section

### Option 2: Start Direct Stream (After Token Creation)

Create your token first, then start streaming directly on Yoink:

1. **Create your token** without attaching a stream
2. **Visit your token page** after creation
3. **Click "Start Stream"** button
4. **Set up your streaming software** with provided stream key
5. **Go live** directly on Yoink platform

**Benefits:**
- Full control over streaming platform
- Native Yoink chat integration
- Built-in trading widgets during stream
- Platform-specific features and monetization

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important Choice:** If you attach a Twitch stream during token creation, you **cannot** start a direct Yoink stream later. Choose your streaming method carefully!
{% endhint %}

## Setting Up Twitch Integration

### Requirements
- Active Twitch account
- Live streaming capability
- Stable internet connection

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Prepare Your Twitch Stream**
   - Ensure you're live or ready to go live
   - Set an engaging stream title
   - Have good audio/video quality

2. **During Token Creation**
   - Toggle "Attach a Twitch stream" to ON
   - Enter your stream title (this becomes your token's main title)
   - Paste your Twitch channel URL
   - Click refresh to verify you're live

3. **Auto-Detection Features**
   - System checks if you're currently streaming
   - Pulls live thumbnail for token preview
   - Detects stream title and viewer count
   - Verifies channel exists and is accessible

4. **Complete Token Creation**
   - Review all details carefully
   - Create your token with stream attached
   - Share your token page to drive viewers to your stream

### Stream Management

**When You're Live:**
- Token page shows "LIVE" badge
- Real-time thumbnail updates
- Direct link to your Twitch stream
- Trading activity may increase during streams

**When You're Offline:**
- Token page shows last stream thumbnail
- "OFFLINE" status indicator
- Link still directs to your Twitch channel
- Normal trading continues

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Stream Not Detected"**
- Verify you're currently live on Twitch
- Check your Twitch URL format is correct
- Ensure your stream is public, not private
- Try refreshing the detection button

**"Invalid Stream URL"**
- Use format: `https://twitch.tv/yourusername`
- Don't include extra parameters or paths
- Verify your username spelling is correct

**"Stream Goes Offline During Creation"**
- Token creation will still complete successfully
- Stream integration remains active for future streams
- Live detection will resume when you stream again
